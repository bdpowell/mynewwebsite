Marketing & Design Internship

I started wokring with the Keweenaw Community Foundation in April of 2016.

I have always been interested in nonprofit work. KCF, for me, was the perfect fit because not only do I get to be a part of a great team, but I also become more aware of a great community. As a student, this is so important to me because—even though I am far from home—it helps to keep me grounded.

During my time with KCF, I completed various design and content projects such as, a new brochure, informational handouts for their Planned Giving Initiative, web page content for the different funds pages on the website, and web banners for the homepage of the website.
