Study Abroad Summer 2016

During the summer of 2016 I participated in a Michigan Tech faculty lead study abroad to Lima, Perú. This was a six-week long excursion, in which I took 3 classes and lived in country. The courses I took were a basic spanish grammar course, a Peruvian culture course, and an exploration course.

My classmates and I all lived in the Miraflores district of Lima with one Tech roommate and a host family. Not only did this study abroad make it possible for me to earn my International Spanish Minor, but it also let me experience a different way of living.

Below are some more photos from my time in Perú. The most memorable of the excursions, for me, was our trip to Machu Picchu. You can also read more about my trip on my blog.
