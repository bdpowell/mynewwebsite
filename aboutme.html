About me

Hi! My name is Brooke. I am a senior Scientific and Technical Communication (STC) major at Michigan Technological University in Houghton, MI. I have a passion for problem solving and connecting with people. I love to puzzle out complex documentation and rebuild it so that it can reach its intended audience.

In my free time I read, everything. Books, magazines, blogs, you name it. I enjoy seeing others writing styles and how they set up their work. I am also an amature photographer, pet lover, and love to go for hikes in the woods. I also really enjoy playing video games like, League of Legends and Battlefield with my friends.

On this website you will find documentation of the work I have done as an STC undergrad here at Tech. Here you can find my resume and LinkedIn page. I would love to hear from you, if you have any questions about freelance work you need done. You can find my contact information below.
